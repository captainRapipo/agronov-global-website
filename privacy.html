Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

AGRONOV Global ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal and business data. This policy applies to all visitors and partnership applicants.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity & Contact Data: Name, email, and phone number.
  • Business Data: Amazon storefront links, catalog size, and distribution methods.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance experience and analyze traffic.

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for core site functionality.
  • Analytics: Tools such as Google Analytics to understand usage.
  • Control: You may set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features may be affected.

4. How We Use & Share Your Data

We use your data to assess partnership applications and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information. Data is only shared with trusted third-party providers (hosting, CRM) who are contractually bound to confidentiality.

5. Data Security & Retention

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its original purpose or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights & Privacy Disclosures

Under applicable laws including Wyoming state law, Nevada NRS 603A, GDPR, and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. While we do not currently sell data, you may submit opt-out requests to: amazon@agronovglobal.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
